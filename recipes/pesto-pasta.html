Pesto Pasta

(Source)

Description

A dish easy to make and full of flavor. Tastes good hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
  4. This versatile pesto pasta recipe is easy to customize by adding your favorite protein, if you like. Chicken, sausage, and chickpeas are all delicious mix-ins.
