Healthy Coconut Oatmeal

(Source)

Description

An easy to prepare and healthy breakfast or high-energy snack. Made with coconut, raisins, cranberries and walnuts.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups plain or vanilla soy milk
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • ¼ cup pure maple syrup
  • ⅓ cup raisins
  • ⅓ cup dried cranberries
  • ⅓ cup sweetened flaked coconut
  • ⅓ cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt (Optional)
  • 3 tablespoons honey (Optional)

Steps

  1. Pour the milk and salt into a saucepan, and bring to a boil.
  2. Stir in the oats, maple syrup, raisins, and cranberries. Return to a boil, then reduce heat to medium. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in walnuts and coconut, and let stand until it reaches your desired thickness.
  4. Spoon into serving bowls, and top with yogurt and honey, if desired.
